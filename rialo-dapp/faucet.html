Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need to connect Twitter and Discord?

We require social account verification to prevent abuse of the faucet and ensure tokens are distributed to real users. Account age verification helps prevent creation of fake accounts just to claim tokens.

How often can I claim from the faucet?

Each wallet address can claim 100 RLO once every 24 hours. There is also a daily limit for the entire faucet which resets at midnight UTC.

What can I do with RIALO tokens?

RIALO tokens allow you to participate in governance voting, stake for rewards, and access exclusive features in the RIALO ecosystem.

I'm having trouble connecting my accounts. What should I do?

Make sure you're logged into your Twitter and Discord accounts in your browser. If issues persist, try refreshing the page or contact our support team on Discord.
